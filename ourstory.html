Our Donut Journey

From a small family bakery to a gourmet donut revolution

1950

Our grandparents open a small bakery in downtown, specializing in traditional donuts.

1975

The second generation takes over, introducing new flavors and expanding to multiple locations.

2000

We embrace the artisanal food movement, focusing on high-quality, locally-sourced ingredients.

2010

Launch of our first gourmet donut line, featuring unique flavor combinations and artistic designs.

2020

Introduction of our plant-based donut range, catering to the growing demand for vegan options.

Today

Continuing to innovate with molecular gastronomy techniques and interactive donut experiences.
